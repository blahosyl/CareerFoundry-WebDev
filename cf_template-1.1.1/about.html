About me

Introduction

As a trained linguist, my main strength is quickly coming to grips with technical issues – be it the description of a deployment pipeline or a security policy – and relaying relevant information to customers or internal stakeholders with no affinity for the jargon.

I thrive in dynamically developing environments, and relish the challenge to learn new skills and to constantly improve procedures, simply for the satisfaction of a job well done, rather than external rewards.

Having lived in 6 different countries and dealt with colleagues and clients from all over the world, I have developed a great sensitivity for cultural differences and not taking any kind of “common knowledge” for granted.

My Skills

  • Technical writing
  • Web development
  • Typesetting (LaTeX)
  • Editing, proofreading
  • Research
